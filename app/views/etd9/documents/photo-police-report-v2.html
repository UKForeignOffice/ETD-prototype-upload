Add your police report

As you have reported your passport as stolen and are in a country that issues a police report for this, you need to provide a copy of it.

If you are using a phone or tablet that can take photos, you can take a photo of each page of your police report and upload it now.

How to photograph your police report

  • Lay it flat on a table or for better results against a wall
  • Use the upload a photo button
  • Photograph each page
  • Make sure references are readable
  • No flash if possible
  • Position the phone parallel to the document
  • Use both hands to keep your phone steady

If you need more detail, you can watch the how to photograph a document video (optional)
