Steps in this application

1.

Your details

First you'll enter your details

Enter your details >

2.

Your photo - optional

If you choose to have your emergency passport delivered to you, you need to upload a digital photo. If you're picking up your emergency passport in the consulate, then a photo upload is optional (it could speed up your application). We'll show you how to take a photo for your emergency passport and upload it.

3.

Other documents - optional

After that you'll have the option of uploading other documents if you need to. For example if you have a police report for a stolen passport, you can take a photo of it and upload it.

4.

Book a courier or consulate appointment

After that you can either book an appointment at a consulate of your choice or book a secure courier service to deliver your emergency passport to you.

5.

Pay

If you choose to have your emergency passport delivered to you, you need to pay online or have a friend pay online. If you choose to collect in a consulate, you can pay online, have a friend pay online or pay at the consulate. The emergency passport is £100 online (or the equivalent in local currency in the consulate). The courier service is an additonal £20 online.
