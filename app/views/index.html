Document upload versions:

Note: Users were worried and confused by the mention of document upload and proof of residency in the introduction pages. This proposes only adding a document upload step dynamically if a) they have a police report AND are in a country that issues them (i.e. not Netherlands) and/or b) want to return to where they are and need to provide proof of address.
